Welcome to E-KORS

We, E-Kors Private Limited, specialize in pioneering the future of electrical safety through our expertise in earthing and lightning arrestor solutions. As a professionally managed company, we are dedicated to providing cutting-edge earthing systems that ensure the safety of property, people, and equipment. Additionally, we offer comprehensive solutions in solar EPC and I&C, catering to the complete spectrum of solar energy projects.

Earthing & Lightning Arresters

At E-Kors Private Limited, we provide advanced earthing and lightning arrester solutions to safeguard property, people, and equipment. Using cutting-edge technology and industry best practices, we ensure effective fault current dissipation and protection from lightning strikes.

Solar Solutions

At E-Kors Private Limited, we offer end-to-end solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) and I&C (Installation and Commissioning) solutions. From the initial design phase to final installation and operational setup, we provide comprehensive services to ensure efficient and optimal performance of your solar energy systems.

Solar BOS Material

We provide a comprehensive range of Solar BOS (Balance of System) materials essential for the efficient and reliable operation of solar energy systems. Our BOS offerings include all components required aside from the solar panels themselves, such as inverters, wiring, mounting structures, and monitoring systems.
